What is Javaniko Calendar?

Javaniko Calendar is a platform where you can create projects using Scrum, with a workspace to link your projects, related sprints for each project, and the exclusive option in the platform, get a Niko Niko calendar for each sprint to monitor the team satisfaction with the done work. There is also available an option to see the statistics of workspaces, proyects and sprints to analyze all of the content.

Express your emotions

Thanks to the exclusive functionality of the application, you can describe how was your day using emojis in our Niko Niko Calendars.

See progress thanks to sprints

Use sprints to keep full track of tasks and Burn-Down graph to see story points remaining.

Monitor your work thanks to our graphs for workspaces, projects, sprints and custom graphs for each user.
